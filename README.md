5. Key Insights

1)	January and March recorded the highest order volume, indicating strong customer demand during these periods. 
2)	Revenue generation was highest during January, March, August, and October, showing strong business performance in these months. 
3)	Traffic conditions significantly impacted delivery efficiency, with higher delays observed during jammed and high-traffic situations. 
4)	Scooters, bikes, and cars demonstrated better delivery efficiency and faster order fulfilment. 
5)	Most customer ratings fall within the 4–5 category, indicating high customer satisfaction and positive service quality. 
6)	Chinese and Italian cuisines showed the highest customer demand and order frequency. 
7)	Mumbai and Chennai generated the highest revenue among all cities analysed. 
8)	Weather conditions such as sunny and cloudy environments contributed to faster and smoother delivery operations. 
9)	The analysis highlights opportunities to improve delivery efficiency, optimize operational planning, and enhance customer experience through data-driven decision-making. 


6. Recommendations

1)	Implement route optimization and smart delivery allocation systems to reduce delays caused by traffic congestion. 
2)	Increase the usage of efficient delivery vehicles such as scooters and bikes during peak traffic hours to improve delivery performance. 
3)	Introduce promotional campaigns and customer retention strategies during low-demand months to maintain consistent order volume and revenue. 
4)	Focus marketing and expansion strategies on high-performing cities such as Mumbai and Chennai to maximize business growth. 
5)	Improve customer satisfaction by continuously monitoring low ratings and addressing operational issues affecting delivery quality. 
6)	Utilize customer cuisine preferences to optimize restaurant partnerships, personalized recommendations, and targeted promotions.
